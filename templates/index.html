How CleanTrack AI Works

Simple, transparent, and effective sanitation reporting

1

Citizen Reports

Citizen uploads a photo of the garbage hotspot with GPS location and description.

2

AI Verifies

YOLOv8 analyzes the image and determines if garbage is present, filtering invalid reports.

3

Authority Acts

Sanitation authority reviews the verified complaint and dispatches a cleanup team.

4

Resolution Confirmed

Authority uploads proof of cleanup. Status changes to Resolved and citizen is notified.
